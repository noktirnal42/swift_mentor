## Can You Use Both LemonSqueezy AND Gumroad?

### YES! Here's How:

**Strategy:**
1. Create products on BOTH platforms
2. List both payment options on GitHub:
   ```markdown
   ## Purchase License
   
   ### Personal License - $20
   - 30-day free trial
   - Perpetual license
   
   [Buy with LemonSqueezy](link) (Recommended - supports developer)
   [Buy with Gumroad](link) (Alternative)
   ```

3. Both generate license keys automatically
4. Both email keys to customers
5. You get paid from both (separate deposits)

### Why Use Both?
- ✅ Customers can choose preferred payment method
- ✅ Backup if one has issues
- ✅ Test which converts better
- ✅ Gumroad for quick setup, LemonSqueezy for lower fees

### Downsides:
- ⚠️ Two sets of analytics to track
- ⚠️ Two payout accounts to manage
- ⚠️ Slightly more complex to manage

### My Recommendation:
**Start with LemonSqueezy only** (lower fees, better for software)

If you want a backup or want to test, add Gumroad later.
